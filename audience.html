Audience.fm

Audience.fm was a tech. startup I co-founded with Hedi Smida and Tarek Ibrahim in 2013.

We built a social platform that helped fans engage with their favorite artists, and a music analytics tool which helped artists and brands target fans.

We were incubated by Angelpad (ranked #1 startup incubator by MIT) and released a music analytics tool.

We reached promising initial milestones including winning Midem's 2013 startup award (the most covetted award in the music startup space) and running pilots with top bands and labels.

We ultimately decided to pivot after failing to raise institutional capital. R.I.P.
