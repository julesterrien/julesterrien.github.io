Moonkeys Music

Moonkeys Music (now LesAutres) is a Paris-based music label I co-founded with Martin Toloton in 2009.

The company focuses on talent management and music publishing and has signed a number of artists to its roster including Emily Loizeau, A-Wa, Igit, Auden, Namaste and Christine.

The company has also signed deals with all major French labels including Universal, EMI, Sony BMG, etc.
