Moonkeys Music


Moonkeys Music (now LesAutres) is a Paris-based music label I co-founded with Martin Toloton in 2009.

The company focuses on music production, talent management and content publishing. We've signed a number of artist including Emily Loizeau, A-Wa, Igit, Auden, Namaste and Christine.

The company has also signed deals with all major French labels including Universal, EMI and Sony BMG.
