MindBits

MindBits was a mobile-first note-taking & link management tool the Audience.fm team and I built after a pivot in 2013.

Although MindBits was ultimately shut down, I decided to keep the idea alive and am now building Stackle.

MindBits let users:
- create notes
- bundle notes together
- share notes with friends
- access a dashboard with notes and links
- access a feed to discover shared notes
