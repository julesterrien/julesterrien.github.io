Stackle

Stackle is an app I've been building on my free time.
It's a place to organize and share all the ideas and related links you come across.

As a user, you can:
- create "stacks" to list & organize notes & links
- invite others to contribute to a stack
- keep stacks private or make them public
- add comments on posts and star your favorite ones
- follow other users and/or their stacks
- access a dashboard to find & organize stacks
- find stacks based on topics
- full text search
- access a feed with new stacks & relevant posts
- receive relevant notifications

I use it to keep and share information on various interests such as my favorite things to do in my home city of Paris, my notes on startup fundraising, my top resources for product design, and more...

The back-end is built in Ruby/Rails with a JSON api and the front-end is built using React.js.

Learn more at stackle.it
