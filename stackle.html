Stackle


Stackle is an app I've been building on my free time. It's a place to organize and share all ideas and related links.

Users can create lists (called 'stacks') to organize notes & links,
and invite others to contribute.
Stacks can be private or shared publicly.
Users can also use a feed to discover stacks shared by others.

I use it to keep and share information on various interests such as my favorite things to do in my home city of Paris, my notes on startup fundraising, my top resources for product design, etc...

The back-end & REST API are built in Ruby/Rails and the front-end is built using React.js.

Learn more at stackle.it
